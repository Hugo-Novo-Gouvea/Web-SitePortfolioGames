ABOUT ME

Hello, my name is Hugo Novo Gouvea, and I am passionate about video games. Ever since I can remember, I’ve been holding a controller while trying to defeat some boss. Over time, this love has only grown, leading me to dive deeper into this world and learn more about it. In 2017, I entered college to study Computer Engineering, as I wanted to work in the technology field, but I still hadn’t found my path. It was only in 2019 that I founded, along with some friends, a study group focused on game development at college, called GRAFHO. Finally, I felt like I was having fun with something. Because of this, I started studying and developing games. Here you can check out some of the games I’ve been developing!
